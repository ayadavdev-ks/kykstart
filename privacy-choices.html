Your Privacy Choices at Kykstart.com

At Nuosoft LLC ("Kykstart.com"), we are committed to transparency and giving you control over your personal information. This page outlines your data privacy rights and provides a simple way for you to exercise them.

How to Submit a Request

To exercise any of the rights listed below, please follow this two-step process:

  1. Send an email to: privacy@kykstart.com
  2. In the subject line, please state the right you wish to exercise. For example: "Data Deletion Request" or "Request to Know My Personal Information".

To protect your privacy, we will need to verify your identity before processing your request. We will do this by asking you to confirm certain information we already have on file for you.

Your Privacy Rights Explained

1. The Right to Know

You have the right to request details about the personal information we have collected about you, including the categories of information and the purposes for which we use it.

How to Request: Send an email with the subject line "Request to Know My Information".

2. The Right to Correct

If you believe any of the personal information we hold about you is inaccurate, you have the right to request that it be corrected.

How to Request: Send an email with the subject line "Data Correction Request" and specify in the body of the email what information you believe is inaccurate and what the correct information should be.

3. The Right to Delete

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information from our records, subject to certain legal exceptions (for example, if we need the data to complete a transaction or comply with a legal obligation).

How to Request: Send an email with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

4. The Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing

You have the right to direct us not to sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

How to Request: Send an email with the subject line "Opt-Out of Sharing Request".

5. The Right to Restrict Use of Sensitive Information

You have the right to limit our use and disclosure of your sensitive personal information (e.g., race, religion) to that which is necessary to perform the services you have requested.

How to Request: Send an email with the subject line "Restrict Sensitive Information Use".

Our Process & Timeline

Acknowledgment: We will confirm receipt of your request within 10 business days.

Verification: We will contact you to verify your identity. Please respond promptly to ensure we can process your request in a timely manner.

Response: We will aim to respond substantively to your request within 45 calendar days. If we require more time (up to an additional 45 days), we will inform you in writing of the reason and extension period.

If you have any questions about this process, please feel free to contact us at privacy@kykstart.com.
